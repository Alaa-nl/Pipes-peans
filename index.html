About Us

Welcome to Pipes & Beans

Your cozy neighborhood coffee shop in the heart of Amsterdam. We're more than just a cafe - we're a community hub where locals and visitors alike come together over great coffee, delicious food, and good vibes.

Enjoy our comfortable seating, free WiFi, and collection of board games and video games. Whether you're looking for a quiet place to work, catch up with friends, or just relax with a good book, Pipes & Beans is your home away from home.
